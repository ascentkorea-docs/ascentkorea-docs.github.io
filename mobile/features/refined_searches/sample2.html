      Barely good enough, would not buy again.6 years ago
      This is the barest minimal phone needed for the sort of communication we expect today. It is best used for voice and SMS (texting) only. It does have a good ...More
      This is the barest minimal phone needed for the sort of communication we expect today. It is best used for voice and SMS (texting) only. It does have a good fit and finish when in new condition. It needs charging daily if you use it moderately. I wouldn't roam the Web with it. It's so slow! I purchased two of these from the same Ebay dealer within a week. The phones looked good but one never would connect with Ting, which is a T-Mobile partner. That same phone also refused to connect with T-Mobile. Two techs at a T-Mobile office tried and failed to make it work. I kept the one that worked with Ting but it is distinctly inferior in speed and handling to my old geezer phone - an old ATT Samsung with a mechanical keyboard. I can still buy duplicates of that old Samsung phone for around $10-20 and it works better than this model. I wouldn't waste time using it for Web access either. But there's still plenty of money to be saved NOT using a phone for internet. If you can use a phone that can make voice calls and do texting, this is not the best selection but it's not the worst. It costs way more than the alternative Samsung SGH-A187. I no longer use ATT for my phone company but if you do, it's a good alternative to this phone.
      bigorangeshoes · Reviewed on ebay.com
